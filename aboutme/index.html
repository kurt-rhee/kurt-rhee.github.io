About Me

Data Scientist 4+ years of progressive experience using python to automate and analyze solar engineering tasks and datasets

Bibliophile Favorite books include Brothers Karamazov by Fyodor Dostoyevsky, Stags Leap by Sharon Olds, and The Unbearable Lightness of Being by Milan Kundera
Environmentalist I cook no meat, buy (almost) no plastic at the grocery store and drive 75% electric in my series-hybrid Chevrolet Volt
